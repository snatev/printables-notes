Privacy Policy

Printables Model Reminder

Printables Model Reminder is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal user data off your device.

All comments and reminders you enter into the extension are saved locally in your browser's storage via the Chrome storage.local API. This means your data remains on your device and under your control.

We believe in transparency and respect for your privacy:

  • No data is ever uploaded to our servers
  • No personal information is shared with any third parties
  • No tracking or analytics are implemented in this extension

If you have any questions about this policy or concerns about your privacy while using our extension, please contact the developer at info@snatev.com.
